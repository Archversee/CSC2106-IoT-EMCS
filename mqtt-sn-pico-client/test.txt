Len, the Red-Eyed Wanderer, is a new Anima warrior class on a quest to fulfill a loved one's dream. She wields a one-handed Longsword and a 'Wish-Granting Jewel' secondary. Her core mechanic is a duality of two sword styles: the 'Plum Blossom Sword' for her personal arts and the 'Lost Soul Sword', which channels the inner energy of the Imugi. When these energies resonate, Len can unleash 'Ultimate' sword techniques, culminating in her 6th Job Origin Skill, 'Sky Splitting Azure Dragon Sword'. Her Link Skill is 'Rigid Body', offering 4% damage reduction.

Len’s early gameplay establishes high mobility. 1st Job skills include 'Clear Breeze Step' (double jump) and 'Plum Blossom Secret', a unique skill that acts as an upward jump and allows her to float in the air. 2nd Job enhances this with 'Plum Blossom Sword 1st Form: Gentle Dash', a forward teleport that can be re-input to teleport backwards. This job also introduces the Imugi's power via 'Lost Soul Sword Fundamental: Soul Strike'. This is a passive with a 35% chance to activate when her sword skills hit, unleashing an extra Imugi energy strike.

In 3rd Job, Len’s kit expands with 'Scattering Plums' (tracking petals) and 'Vital Energy' (HP recovery). The key addition is 'Lost Soul Advent', a skill that allows her to chain 'Lost Soul Sword Ultimate' skills in order, starting with 'Splitting Ground'. In 4th Job, she gets her main bossing skill, 'Relentless Slash', a keydown attack. She also gains 'Keen Blade', a summon that fires a moving sword slash, and 'Zero Blade', a crucial 3-second invincibility skill. Her 'Lost Soul Advent' combo expands with 'Void Fang' (4th Job), 'Infinite Blades' (Hyper), and 'Soul Blade' (5th Job).

In 5th Job, Len’s power escalates with two major summons. 'Plum Tree Aroma' summons a tree that activates 'Plum Slash' when Len hits, and 'Blooms' to attack after enough activations. 'Lost Soul Awakening' activates 'Heavenly Fang' on hit, and after 90 hits, unleashes 'Heavenly Punishment'. She also gains 'Plum Blossom Sword Ultimate: Dance of Annihilation', a 5-second keydown with super stance. During this, she can use 'Lost Soul Sword Ultimate' skills with no cast action for a massive burst. Her 6th Job Origin Skill, 'Sky Splitting Azure Dragon Sword: Ascension', is her peak, granting invincibility.
